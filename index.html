Privacy Policy for Lumen

Last updated: March 1, 2026

1. Overview

Lumen is a paid, offline puzzle game developed by One Rose Studios Inc.

We respect your privacy.

2. Information We Collect

Lumen does not collect, store, transmit, or process any personal information.

  • No user accounts
  • No analytics
  • No advertising
  • No tracking technologies
  • No in-app purchases
  • No server communication

The game functions entirely offline.

3. Data Stored on Your Device

Game progress and settings may be stored locally on your device. This information does not leave your device and is not accessible to us.

4. Apple Platform Services

If you download Lumen from the App Store, Apple may collect information in accordance with its own privacy policy. We do not have access to that data.

5. Children’s Privacy

Because we do not collect personal information, we do not knowingly collect data from children.

6. Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted with a revised date.
